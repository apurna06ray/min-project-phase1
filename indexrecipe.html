Veg-biryani

Veg Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with a mix of vegetables, basmati rice, and a blend of rich Indian spices. It's a perfect meal for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cup basmati rice
  2. 1 cup mixed vegitables (carrot, peas, beans, potato)
  3. 1 large onion, sliced
  4. 2 tomatoes, chopped
  5. 2 tbsp biryani masala
  6. 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  7. 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  8. Salt to taste
  9. Fresh coriander and mint leaves

Instruction:

  • Rinse and soak basmati rice for 20 minutes.
  • Heat oil or ghee in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  • Mix in chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  • Add the vegetables, biryani masala, and salt. Stir well.
  • Add soaked rice and enough water to cook. Cover and cook on low flame.
  • Once done, garnish with fresh coriander and mint leaves.
  • Serve hot with raita or salad.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Veg Biryani!
